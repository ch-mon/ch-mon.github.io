Personal

If you look it up on Wikipedia, Curriculum Vitae loosely translates from Latin as course of life. As Wikipedia continues to know, it usually refers to the complete record of someone’s career. However, as much as I believe that work is a crucial part of our lives, for this website, I will refer back to the original meaning. By doing so, I will reach beyond a classical CV and express the course of my life by drawing a more wholesome picture - including important stations and most shaping moments of my life.
