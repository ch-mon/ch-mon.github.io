Life (Curriculum Vitae)

If you look it up on Wikipedia, Curriculum Vitae loosely translates from Latin as course of life. As Wikipedia continues to know, it usually rather refers to the complete record of someone’s career. However, as work is an important part of our lifes, for this page I will refer back to this original meaning. By doing so I will reach beyond the limitations of a CV and express the course of my life by trying to draw a more whole picture - including the important stations and shaping moments of in my life.
